Destroy Rachel Online

[Web, Game]

Come Destroy Online Me!

This project was made as a way to create joy out of my discomfort and frustrations towards the practices and norms in place for how we present ourselves online. For me, that joy was "destroying" my online profiles and public information. Rather than just deleting them, I wanted them to disappear in a glittery poof!

Uses anime.js and Disintegrate.js.
