About

This project utilizes AR to create pop-up cards for a celebratory occasion. I specifically chose to make cards for a birthday and Valentine's Day because nothing is better than celebrating yourself and your loved ones.

This project utilizes Unity.

Process

Below I created two card fronts which would serve as the Image Targets that the AR objects would revolve around.

When the targets are detected, animated objects with a message will appear. This AR aspect functions as the inside of the card. Ideally, the messages would be customizable.

As any graphic can technically serve as an image target, AR has the potential to broaden how the physical space can be utilized to communicate. With the AR camera, even graphics in our environment can hold a hidden message.
